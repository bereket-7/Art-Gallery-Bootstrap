Paintings

Paintings are artworks created by applying various mediums—pigments, oil paint, inks, watercolors, etc.—onto a two-dimensional surface such as a canvas, paper, or board. Paintings range from representational and abstract works of art to religious imagery. The goal of a painting is to express ideas and emotions that the artist has.

Cultural

Cultural paintings are artwork that depict the culture of a particular time or place. These works can include traditional art forms such as murals, illustrations, sketches, and popular art. Cultural paintings often feature images of people, events, customs, values, ideas, and symbols associated with that specific tribe or country.

Artistic

Artistic painting is an expression of emotions, ideas, and experiences created with visual media. It may involve the use of different painting materials such as oil paints, watercolors, acrylics, inks, colored pencils, pastels and other mixed media. Artistic painting may also involve abstract or representational elements as well as creative expression.

Minitry of tourism

The Ministry of Tourism is a government department in ethiopia that works to enhance the travel and tourism industry. The ministry formulates policies and regulations, creates infrastructure and invests heavily in promotional campaigns and events. Its focus is on promoting India as a world-renowned tourist destination internationally, while also facilitating development of the domestic tourism sector.
